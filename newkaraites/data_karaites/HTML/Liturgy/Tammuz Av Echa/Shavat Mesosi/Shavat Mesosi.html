שָׁבַת מְשׂוֹשִׂי וְרַבּוּ יְגוֹנָי:

גָּבַר שֹׁסִי וְעָֽצְמוּ מוֹנָי:

וְנִמְשַׁךְ קִצִּי עַל רֹב עֲוֹנָי:

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

 

Shavat mesosi verabbu yeḡonai

Gavar shosi ve‘atsemu monai

Venimshach ḳits-tsi ‘al rov ‘avonai

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

 

My joy has ceased, and my anguish is great,

My enemy has become strong, and my foes formidable,

The time until my redemption drags out, because of my many sins –

Will Adonai forsake forever?

 

 

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

Will Adonai forsake forever?

 

לְשִׁמְךָ יְקַוּוּ בְּנֵי אֵיתָנִים

בִּבְכִי יָבֹאוּ וּבְתַחֲנוּנִים

תָּשׁוּב תְּרַחֵם תִּכְבֹשׁ זְדוֹנִים:

זְכוֹר לִי צִדְקַת שֹׁמֵר אֱמוּנִים:

פָּנִים בְּפָנִים דִּבֶּר יוָי:

 

Leshimcha yeḳavvu benē etanim

Bivchi yavo’u uvtaḥanunim

Tashuv teraḥem tichbosh zedonim

Zechor li tsidḳat shomer emunim

Panim befanim dibber adonai.

 

The children of the patriarchs put their hope in your name,

The come in tears and in supplications.

Come back, have mercy, and throw out our wanton sins.

Remember, for me, the merit of [Moses], who kept faith,

And Adonai spoke face to face with him.

 

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

Will Adonai forsake forever?

 

מָרוֹם תִּכֵּן שְׁחָקִים בַּזֶּרֶת:

מִידֵי שִׁפְחָה תַּצִּיל גְּבֶרֶת:

וְרַבַּת בָּנִים תּוֹשִׁיב עֲקֶרֶת:

זְכֹר לִי צִדְקַת מַלְאַך עֲתֶרֶת

וְנָתַן הַקְּטֹרֶת לִפְנֵי יוָי:

 

 

Marom tikken sheḥaḳim bazzeret

Midē shifḥa tats-tsil geveret

Verabbat banim toshiv ‘aḳeret

Zechor li tsidḳat mal’ach ‘ateret,

Venatan haḳḳetoret lifnē adonai.

 

O God most high, who has set up the heavens with the span of his hand,

Rescue the mistress from the hand of her slave-woman,

And bring back the barren woman to be a mother of many children.

Remember, for me, the merit of the angel who prayed supplicatively [Aaron],

Who placed the incense in front of Adonai.

 

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

Will Adonai forsake forever?

 

הַבֵּט כִּי עַמְּךָ נִגָּשׂ וְנַעֲנֶה:

בָּזוּי וְשָׁסוּי אָנָה וְאָנֶה:

בְּעֵילָם וּבְאַשּׁוּר וּבִקְצֵה דְדָנֶה:

זְכֹר לִי צִדְקַת מַלְאַךְ מְקַנֵּא:

הַבָּא לִפְנֵי בּוֹא יוֹם יוָי:

Habbet ki ‘ammecha niggas vena‘anē

Bazuy veshasuy ana ve’anē

Be‘elam uv’ash-shur uviḳtsē dedanē

Zechor li tsidḳat mal’ach meḳannē

Habba lifnē bo yom adonai.

 

See that your people is oppressed and abused,

Disgraced and trampled, in every direction,

In Elam and Assyria and outermost Dedan.

Remember, for me, the merit of the angel who was zealous [Elijah],

Who will come before the Day of Adonai.

 

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

Will Adonai forsake forever?

 

רְאֵה כִּי בֵית יַעֲֲקֹב דַּלִּים וְרָשִׁים:

רְאֵה כִּי הַיּוֹם נִכְלָמִים בֹּשִׁים:

רְאֵה כִּי עַל גַּבָּם חָֽרְשׁוּ חֹֽרְשִׁים:

זְכֹר לִי צִדְקַת נֶאֱמָן קְדֹשִׁים:

טֹוב עִם אֲנָשִׁים וְטוֹב עִם יוָי:

 

Re’ē ki vet ya‘aḳov dallim verashim

Re’ē ki hayyom nichlamim boshim

Re’ē ki ‘al gabbam ḥareshu ḥoreshim

Zechor li tsidḳat ne’eman ḳedoshim

Tov ‘im anashim vetov ‘im adonai.

 

See that the House of Jacob is poor and impoverished,

See that today they are shamed and embarrassed,

See that plows have plowed over their backs.

Remember, for me, the merit of the one faithful among the holy [Samuel],

Who was good with men, and good with Adonai.

 

הַלְעוֹלָמִים יִזְנַח אֲדֹנָי:

Hal‘olamim yiznaḥ adonai.

Will Adonai forsake forever?
