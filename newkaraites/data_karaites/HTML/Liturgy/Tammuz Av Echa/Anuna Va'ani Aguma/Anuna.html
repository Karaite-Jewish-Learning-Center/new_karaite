וַאֲנִ֤י ׀ אֵלֶ֣יךָ יְהֹוָ֣ה שִׁוַּ֑עְתִּי וּ֝בַבֹּ֗קֶר תְּֽפִלָּתִ֥י תְקַדְּמֶֽךָּ׃ וַאֲנִ֤י תְפִלָּֽתִי־לְךָ֨ ׀ יְהֹוָ֡ה עֵ֤ת רָצ֗וֹן אֱלֹהִ֥ים בְּרׇב־חַסְדֶּ֑ךָ עֲ֝נֵ֗נִי בֶּאֱמֶ֥ת יִשְׁעֶֽךָ׃

 

Va’ani elecha adonai shivva‘ti, uvabboḳer tefillati teḳademekka. Va’ani tefillati lecha adonai ‘et ratson elohim berov ḥasdecha ‘aneni be’emet yish‘echa.

 

But as for me, unto Thee, O LORD, do I cry, and in the morning doth my prayer come to meet Thee. (Psalms 88:14) But as for me, let my prayer be unto Thee, O LORD, in an acceptable time; O God, in the abundance of Thy mercy, answer me with the truth of Thy salvation. (Psalms 69:14)

 

אֲנוּנָה אֲנִי וַעֲגוּמָה, עֲלוּבָה וַעֲצוּבָה

בְּזוּיָה שְׁפָלָה בְּתוֹךְ גָּלוֹת עֲזוּבָה

גְּעוּלָה נִהְיֵיתִי תַּֽחַת הֱיוֹתִי אֲהוּבָה

נְתָנַֽנִי שֹׁמֵמָה כָּל־הַיּוֹם דָּוָה.

 

Anuna ani va‘aḡuma, ‘aluva va‘atsuva,

Bezuya shefala betoch galut ‘azuva,

Ge‘ula nihyeti taḥat heyoti ahuva,

Netanani shomema kol hayyom dava.

 

 

I am mournful and gloomy, distraught and sad,

Despised and lowly, abandoned in the exile.

I have become rejected, rather than being beloved,

He hath made me desolate and faint all the day. (Echa 1:13)

 

דָּוָה וְזָבָה נִקְרֵאתִי, וּמָאֹס מְאַסְתָּֽנִי

הֱנִיעוֹתַָֽנִי מִמְּקוֹמִי וּנְטַשְׁתָּֽנִי

וְשִׁלַּחְתַּֽנִי מִפָּנֶֽיךָ וּבַאֲרָצוֹת זֵרִיתָֽנִי

כִּי־אַתָּה אֱלֹהֵי מָעוּזִּי לָמָה זְנַחְתָּֽנִי.

 

Dava vezava niḳreti, uma’os me’astani

Heni‘otani mimmeḳomi untashtani

Veshillaḥtani mippanecha uva’aratsot zeritani

Ki atta elohé ma‘uzzi lama zenaḥtani.

 

 

Sick and menstruating have I been called, and You have surely spurned me,

You have moved me from my place, and abandoned me;

And you have sent me away from Your presence, and scattered me around the lands.

For You are the God of my strength; why have You cast me off? (Psalms 43:2)

 

 

זְנַחְתָּֽנִי פְרַצְתָּֽנִי וְגֵרַשְׁתָּנִי מִבֵּית מְעוֹנֶֽךָ

חִתַּתַּֽנִי בַחֲלוֹמוֹת וַתְּבַעֲתַנִּי מֵאֵימוֹת חֶזְיוֹנֶֽךָ

טָהוֹר אִם בִּשְׁאוֹל אֶחְתֹּר לִבְרֹֽחַ מִפָּנֶֽיךָ

גַּם שָׁם יָדְךָ תַנְחֵֽנִי וְתֹאחֲזֵֽנִי יְמִינֶֽךָ.

 

Zenaḥtani feratstani veḡerashtani mibbet me‘onecha

Ḥittatani vaḥalomot vatteva‘atanni me’emot ḥezyonecha

Tahor im bish’ol eḥtor livroaḥ mippanecha

Gam sham yadecha tanḥeni vetoḥazeni yeminecha.

 

 

You have cast me off, burst through into me, and banished me from Your dwelling [the Temple],

You have frightened me with dreams, and terrified me with the dread of Your vision.

O pure One, if I burrow into hell to flee from You,

Even there would Your hand lead me, and Your right hand would hold me. (Psalms 139:10)

 

יְמִינְךָ תִסְעָדֵֽנִי לְהַעֲבִיר כֹּֽבֶד אַשְׁמוֹתָי

כִּי לִמְאֹד הָשְׁפַּֽלְתִּי וְנִתְּקוּ זִמּוֹתָי

לֹחֵץ יִלְחָצֵֽנִי וְהִשְׁפִּיל גֵּאוּת רוֹמְמוֹתַי

וְאַתָּה יוי עַד־מָתָי.

 

Yeminecha tis‘adeni leha‘avir koved ashmotai

Ki lim’od hoshpalti venitteḳu zimmotai

Loḥets yilḥatseni vehishpil ge’ut romemotai,

Ve’atta adonai ‘ad matai.

 

Your right hand, may it hold me to remove the weight of my sins,

For I have greatly sunk down, and my [sinning] plots have been pulled down.

The oppressor oppresses me, and has humiliated my lofty pride –

And You, O Lord, until when?! (Psalms 6:4)

 

מָתַי תּוֹצִיא אָדוֹן לְצֶֽדֶק דִּינָי

נוֹרָא הַאֲזֵן שִׂיחִי וְתַחֲנוּנָי

סְעָדֵֽנִי וְאִוָּשֵֽׁעָה, כִּי לִקְהִלַּת הֲמוֹנָי

אֵלֶֽיךָ נָשָֽׂאתִי אֶת־עֵינָי.

 

Matai totsi adon letsedeḳ dinai

Nora ha’azen siḥi vetaḥanunai

Se‘adeni ve’ivvashe‘a, ki liḳhillat hamonai

Elecha nasati et ‘enai.

 

When will You, O lord, judge my case favorably?

O awesome One, give ear to my speech, my supplication.

Support me, and let me be rescued, for on behalf of my vast congregation,

To You, O Lord, I lift up my eyes. (Psalms 123:1)

 

עֵינַי מְצַפּוֹת לְךָ גֹּאֲלִי אֵלִי

פְּצֵֽנִי מִמִּתְקוֹמְמַי אֱלֹהִים יוי חֵילִי

צוּרִי הַבֵּט וְהַקְשֵׁב לְקוֹל מִלּוּלִי

יוי בֹּֽקֶר תִּשְׁמַע קוֹלִי.

 

‘Enai metsappot lecha go’ali eli,

Petseni mimmitḳomemai elohim adonai ḥeli

Tsuri habbet vehaḳshev leḳol milluli

Adonai boḳer tishma‘ ḳoli.

 

My eyes look forward to You, my redeemer, my God,

Spare me from those that rise up against me, O God, Lord, my strength!

O my Rock, look and listen to the sound of my words,

O Lord, in the morning hear my voice! (Psalms 5:4)

 

 

קוֹלִי שְׁעֵה וְאַל־תִּתְעַלַּם מִתְּחִנָּתִי

רְצֵה יוי לְהַצִּילֵֽנִי וְחֽוּשָׁה לְעֶזְרָתִי

שׁוּר בְּעֶלְבּוֹנִי וּמְחֵה אֶת דִּמְעָתִי

יוי שִׁמְעָה תְפִלָּתִי.

 

Ḳoli she‘é ve’al tit‘allam mitteḥinnati

Retsé adonai lehats-tsileni veḥusha le‘ezrati

Shur be‘elboni umḥé et dim‘ati

Adonai shim‘a tefillati.

 

My voice – heed [it], and do not ignore my supplication,

Find it favorable, O Lord, to rescue me, and hasten to my help!

See my shame, and wipe off my tear,

O Lord, hear my prayer! (Psalms 102:2)

 

תְּפִלָּתִי שִׁמְעָה וְשׁוּב וַעֲנֵֽנִי

יָהּ כְּרֹֽעַ מַַעֲלָלַי אַל תְּדִינֵֽנִי

צַעֲקָתִי תַעֲלֶה לְפָנֶֽיךָ וּבְחַסְדְּךָ חַנְנֵֽנִי

אֲנִי אָמַֽרְתִּי יוי חָנֵּֽנִי.

 

Tefillati shim‘a veshuv va‘aneni

Yah keroa‘ ma‘alalai al tedineni

Tsa‘aḳati ta‘alé lefanecha uvḥasdecha ḥaneneni

Ani amarti adonai ḥonneni.

 

 

My prayer – hear [it], and come answer me,

O Yah, do not judge me in accordance with the wickedness of my deeds.

May my cry ascend before You, and may You show kindness to me with your compassion,

I said: O Lord, show kindness to me! (Psalms 41:5)

 

חָנֵּֽנִי צוּרִי וּתְבַשְּׂרֵֽנִי סָלַֽחְתִּי פְשָׁעֶֽיךָ

קַבְּצֵֽנִי מֵאֲרָצוֹת וְעָלַי יֶהֱמוּ מֵעֶֽיךָ

בְּרֹב יְשׁוּעָתְךָ קַבֵּל תְּפִלַּת מְשַׁוְּעֶֽיךָ
אֱלֹהִים בְּרָב־חַסְדֶּֽךָ עֲנֵֽנִי בֶּאֱמֶת יִשְׁעֶֽךָ.

 

 

Ḥonneni tsuri utvassereni salaḥti fesha‘echa

Ḳabbetseni me’aratsot ve‘alai yehemu me‘echa

Berov yeshu‘atecha ḳabbel tefillat meshavve‘echa

Elohim berov ḥasdecha ‘aneni be’emet yish‘echa.

 

Show kindness to me, O my Rock, and announce to me: “I have forgiven your crimes.”

Gather me in from the lands [of exile], and may Your innards churn for me.

With Your great rescue, accept the prayer of those that beseech You,

O God, in the abundance of Thy mercy, answer me with the truth of Thy salvation. (Psalms 69:14)
